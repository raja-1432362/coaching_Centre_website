Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and admission process

How do I apply for admission at EduExcel Academy?

You can apply for admission by filling out the admission form on our website. After submission, our admission team will contact you to schedule an entrance test and counseling session. Alternatively, you can visit our campus directly for on-spot admission.

What is the fee structure for NEET/JEE courses?

Our fee structure varies depending on the course and duration. The annual fee for our 2-year NEET/JEE program is ₹89,999. We also offer installment plans and scholarships for meritorious students. For detailed fee information, please contact our admission office.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials developed by our expert faculty. This includes theory books, practice workbooks, previous years' question banks, and test series. All materials are regularly updated according to the latest exam patterns and syllabus.

Can I switch from online to offline mode or vice versa?

Yes, we offer flexibility to switch between online and offline modes based on your preference and circumstances. A nominal administrative fee may apply for mode switching. Please discuss your requirements with our academic coordinator.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 90% refund if requested within 15 days of admission. After 15 days but within 30 days, a 50% refund is applicable. No refunds are provided after 30 days of admission. All refund requests must be submitted in writing to the admission office.
